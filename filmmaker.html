Writer / Director / Filmmaker

Havana Hustle

Winner of the Award of Commendation
2017 Canada Shorts - Canadian and International Short Film Festival 

Official Selection 
2018 Women in Film & TV International Showcase (one of 26 films representing women filmmakers from around the world)

During a heat wave, a Canadian girl finds herself at a high stakes poker game with the heads of the Havana underworld. Armed with a trick she learned from an old friend, she stakes her life and her freedom for a chance to get information on a ghost: a loved one presumed dead, for whom she has been relentlessly searching.

Vancouver Asian Film Festival Commercial Contest Winner
